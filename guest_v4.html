Bali Immigration Office (Renon)
Official immigration office. Go in person. Bring passport + copy, 2 photos, filled form. Open Mon–Fri 8am–3pm. Can be slow — go early.
⚡
Indo Visa Agency (Seminyak)
Trusted local agent. They handle everything for you — same day or next day. ~$35 USD all-in. 5 min from the villa. Just bring your passport.
📍 5 min away
⭐ Easiest option
💻
Online via evisa.imigrasi.go.id
Indonesia's official e-visa portal. Can be done online but website can be slow. Allow 3–5 days for processing online.
⚠️ After 60 days, you must leave Indonesia and re-enter for a new VOA. Most people do a "visa run" to Singapore or KL — cheap and easy. Ask Sofia if you need help.
